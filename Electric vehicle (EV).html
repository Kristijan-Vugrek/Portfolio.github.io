Electric vehicle (EV)

Dashboard and visualization of number of electric vehicles registerd in Croatia in year 2022 and number of charging stations.

About the project

In my initial foray into independent projects, I delved into the dynamic landscape of electric vehicles (EVs) in Croatia.
This dashboard project was motivated by the quest to answer fundamental questions and uncover insights, such as:

- What is the real-time count of registered electric vehicles in Croatia?
- How does the distribution of electric vehicles vary across different regions?
- How many charging stations are operational, and where are they located?
- What is the ratio of registered electric vehicles to charging stations in each county of Croatia?

Dive into the Python implementation for data scraping, Excel for data cleaning, SQL for querying and Tableau for visualization to uncover the intricacies of Croatia's electric vehicle ecosystem.
